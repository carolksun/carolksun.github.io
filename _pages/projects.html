Filtering Cost Effective Ad Requests Using Early Delivery Stack Features

Summer 2019, OpenX

Using gateway features from ad requests, I implemented and tuned machine learning models, such as logistic regression, neural networks, and boosted trees. The prototype model provided proof of concept to preserve 99% of the revenue while removing over 6% of the total ad requests, enabling the company to save over $100,000 per year.
